### Action item (test hygiene, not a code bug)

The planet-surface tests are **not asset-independent** — they
`FileNotFoundError` rather than skip when their assets are absent.
Consider a `pytest.importorskip`-style guard (or
`@pytest.mark.skipif(not os.path.isfile(...))`) so a clone without the
licensed planet assets reports them as *skipped*, not *failed*.  Every
other asset-dependent suite in the repo already tolerates absent
assets; these new ones regressed that convention.
