Worth checking whether `stop()` releases the pyglet `Player`, the `arcade.Sound` stream, or the FFmpeg subprocess texture buffer. Not a perf regression — pure memory.

- **Warp-zone soaks remain the memory dominant** — Meteor +705 MB, Lightning +999 MB, Gas +609 MB over 2 min each (within thresholds, but RSS baseline still climbs ~300 MB → ~3 800 MB across the suite, then jumps to 4 475 MB on the failing test).
